About
Dr Ingrid Burkett is a Social Design Fellow at CSI. She combines a background in social work, economic development, business and graphic design into her work as social designer: designing and co-designing processes, products and knowledge that deepen social impact and facilitate social innovation. She has contributed to the design of services, policy and processes in a diversity of fields, including frontline social, health and disability services, community development, local economic development, social investment, social enterprise and social procurement.
Managing Director, Knode Social Design Fellow at CSI
